# Project key fields
# Format: key|Label|Prompt for extraction
title|Title| What is the name of the project?
scope|Project Scope|What kind of work is being done?
participants|Participants|Who are the people and parties involved?
date|Date|When was the report submitted?
quoted|Quoted Price|What price was quoted for the project?
invoice|Invoice Amount|How much was invoiced for the project?
findings|Findings|What key findings, issues, or risks are documented?
recommendations|Recommendations|What actions or recommendations are documented?
gaps|Known Gaps|What important information is missing or uncertain?
